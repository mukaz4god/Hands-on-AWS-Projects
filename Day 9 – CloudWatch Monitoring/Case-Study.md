# Case Study – Monitoring and Alerting for Production Infrastructure

**Business Scenario** <br/>
KFJ Solution, a SaaS company has deployed a highly available application (Days 6–8). However, they currently lack visibility into system health and performance.

**Without monitoring:**<br/>
- Failures go unnoticed
- Performance issues impact users
- No alerts are triggered during incidents

The company needs real-time monitoring, alerting, and dashboards to ensure operational visibility.

**Your Task:**<br/>
As a Cloud Engineer / DevOps Engineer, you are responsible for implementing:

- CloudWatch metrics and alarms
- EC2 detailed monitoring
- Dashboards for visualization
- SNS notifications for alerting
