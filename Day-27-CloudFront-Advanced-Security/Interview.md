# Day 35 – CloudFront Advanced Security

# Interview Ready Wording

## Question: Why place CloudFront in front of S3?

**Answer:**  
CloudFront improves global performance through edge caching and adds security controls such as HTTPS enforcement, signed URLs, geo-restrictions, and origin protection.

---

## Question: What is Origin Access Control?

**Answer:**  
Origin Access Control allows CloudFront to securely access a private S3 bucket while preventing users from accessing the bucket directly.

---

## Question: Why use signed URLs?

**Answer:**  
Signed URLs allow controlled, time-limited access to private content. They are useful for premium downloads, customer-specific content, and temporary secure access.

---

## Question: What is geo-restriction used for?

**Answer:**  
Geo-restriction controls access by country and is commonly used for licensing, regulatory, or business restrictions.
